Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Sunset Adventures?

Take the Trans-Canada highway to the Burgeo Turnoff (The Caribou Trail). Once in Burgeo, we are just off the Reach Road in an area outside town known as Aaron's Arm. You'll see our building on the right as you pull in.

Do I need to bring anything?

We recommend sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, and appropriate footwear that you don't mind getting wet. We provide everything else. Don't forget your smartphone or camera to take pictures.

How many people can you accommodate?

We can take up to 12 people on tour. Contact us to determine which patrons can go in single vs. double kayaks.

How long does the tour last?

The tour runs for approximately 3 hours. It may go a little longer depending on wind and other environmental conditions.
